About

The Championnat du Monde de Lancer d'Avion en Papier is an international paper airplane throwing competition organized by the Rotary Club of Mérignac. The 2025 edition is scheduled for May 15th at the Complexe Sportif Daniel Colombier in Mérignac, featuring participants from 14 countries and a total of 300 competitors. Proceeds from the event will support environmental initiatives.
by Rotary Mérignac
